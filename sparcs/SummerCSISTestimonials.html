Siena CS Alumni Testimonials

“Having always had an interest in modern computer technologies, I decided to take a gamble and see what the world of Computer Science and programming is all about. After taking CSIS 110, and CSIS 120, I was hooked. Programming is like being given a box full of tools and unlimited material to build whatever you could possibly desire to be spilled out onto a computer screen. By the end of CSIS 120 I was programming a java based emulator for the classic video game "Pac-Man", and that summer the Computer Science professors at Siena College were offering me a paying position on a Research Team for Artificial Intelligence programming. Finding my niche in the world of Computer Sciences has provided me not only with joy for the field I intend to make a career out of but also opportunities that I likely wouldn't have received anywhere else."

- Zachary Witter, Siena College Computer Science Major 2014  

“This year I have taken both CSIS 110 & CSIS120 and they are just great! The class has a logical feel to it compared to other non-CSIS classes. It is a nice change of pace, and the coursework is enjoyable. I wish I had tried CS classes sooner because I am now finishing my senior year at Siena.  I have enjoyed the CS so much that I am currently looking for ways to continue my computer science education after graduation. In my bit of work experience, I found background knowledge in your major is assumed - you begin to add value when you offer something different. A Computer Science minor provides a great start to setting yourself apart.”

– Christopher Ferracane, Siena College Finance Major 2013
